# Smart Curriculum Activity & Attendance App

## Project Title
Smart Curriculum Activity & Attendance App

---

## Project Description
The Smart Curriculum Activity & Attendance App is an intelligent academic management system designed to streamline attendance tracking and curriculum activity monitoring within educational institutions. The application helps faculty manage attendance records efficiently while enabling students to view their attendance status and participate in academic activities. The system reduces manual work, improves accuracy, and provides real-time insights into student participation.

---

## Objectives
- To automate the attendance management process.
- To maintain accurate student attendance records.
- To monitor curriculum and co-curricular activities.
- To reduce paperwork and manual errors.
- To provide real-time attendance tracking and reporting.
- To improve communication between students, faculty, and administrators.

---

## Problem Statement
Traditional attendance management systems rely heavily on manual record keeping, which can be time-consuming and prone to errors. Educational institutions require a smart solution that can efficiently manage attendance and curriculum activities while providing accurate reports and analytics.
